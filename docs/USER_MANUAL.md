# FalaMadeira: Your Guide to Mastery

Welcome to **FalaMadeira**, the most immersive way to learn the Portuguese of Madeira. This manual will guide you through our unique methodology and app features.

## 1. The Learning Philosophy
FalaMadeira isn't just about grammar; it's about **culture and connection**. Our curriculum is designed to take you from zero to conversational in 6 months, focusing on the specific phonetic nuances and vocabulary of the Madeira archipelago.

## 2. Your Daily Ritual
- **The Dashboard:** Every day, you'll see a featured lesson. We recommend following the sequence, but you can always revisit past days.
- **Streak & XP:** Consistency is key. Your streak tracks consecutive days of practice, while XP measures your total effort.

## 3. The Curriculum
- **6-Month Roadmap:** Each month focuses on a different theme (e.g., Month 1: Foundations, Month 4: Island Life).
- **Lesson Structure:** 
    - **Context:** Why this lesson matters.
    - **Patterns:** The core building blocks.
    - **Vocabulary:** Essential words.
    - **Video:** Real-world examples.
- **Quizzes:** Every lesson ends with a challenge. Pass it to unlock the next day.

## 4. AI Practice: Your 24/7 Tutor
This is the heart of FalaMadeira.
- **Natural Conversation:** Don't just type—**speak**. Use the microphone icon to practice your pronunciation.
- **Context-Aware:** Your tutor knows exactly which lesson you're on and will guide you through the specific patterns of that day.
- **Interactive Help Mode:** Stuck or confused about an app feature? Click the **Help (?)** icon in any AI Practice session. Your tutor will switch to "Guide Mode" and explain exactly how to use the current screen or any other part of the app.
- **Listening:** Tap the "Listen" button on any AI message to hear the perfect Madeiran accent.

## 5. Review & Customization
- **Review Mode:** Found in the Learning tab. Once a lesson is completed, you can move it into your custom review order.
- **Tutor Selection:** Choose a tutor that matches your learning style in Settings. Whether you want the energy of Maria or the traditional wisdom of Ana, we have a partner for you.

## 6. Premium Benefits
While the core curriculum is free, **Premium** unlocks:
- **Unlimited Voice Practice:** No daily caps on conversation.
- **Advanced Dialect Training:** Deep dives into regional variations.
- **Offline Mode:** (Coming Soon) Practice anywhere on the island.
