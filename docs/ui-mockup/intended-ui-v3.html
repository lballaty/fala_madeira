🫱 Culture
REGISTER · WHO IS “YOU”?
tu · você · o senhor / a senhora
With the cleaner and tradespeople, warm-formal wins: use “o senhor / a senhora” or just the verb without a pronoun. “Tu” is for friends and kids; jumping to it too early feels off. Madeirans forgive every grammar mistake — register mistakes they remember.
INDIRECTNESS · HOW TO ASK
“Queria…” beats “Quero…”
Requests soften: “Queria uma bica” (I'd like…) not “Quero” (I want). On the phone, open with “Olhe, desculpe…” (“look, sorry…”) before asking anything — it's oil for the conversation, not an actual apology.
Cultural notes attach to situations — you'll meet these exactly where they matter.
